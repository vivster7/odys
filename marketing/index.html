Keeping It Simple

Describe anything with boxes, arrows, and text.

Boxes

Command (⌘) + click to add a box.

(Ctrl on Windows)

Arrows

Select a box, then command (⌘) + click another.

(Ctrl on Windows)

Text

Double click to add text.

Don't work in isolation. Keep knowledge accessible to your entire team.

Tangible Abstractions

Engineers love abstractions. But they incur a heavy congnitive tax. Visuals make invented concepts like UserAccessScopes more accessible.

Spatial Relationships

Systems are dynamic. It's difficult to keep track of all the moving pieces. Diagrams keep you organized.

Shared Mental Model

It takes months to ramp up on a new codebase. Reading source code to build a mental model is slow and error prone. Share crucial concepts to onboard new teammates quickly.
